## About the Collection

This site was generated as part of the SLIS 5030: Information Organization coursework. The collection includes 15 images that carry personal significance to me and my siblings, and reflect where we grew up together. The locations of each photo vary and include:  

- our family home
- our grandparents' homes
- shared, community locations
